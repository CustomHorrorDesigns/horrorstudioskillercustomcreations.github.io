About Us

-

Welcome to Horror Studios Killer Custom Creations. Your one stop spot for unique, handmade horror collectables. Perfect for Horror Fans, collectors, and anyone looking to add a touch of retro fright to their space. At Horror Studios Killer Custom Creations, we craft one of a kind horror themed items inspired anywhere from the classic 80's movies, to todays latest horror hits. We craft handmade collectables that'll make your spine tingle. From creepy decor to collectable figurines, each piece is made with care and attention to detail. Wheather you're a lifelong horror fan or just love the darker side of design,our work is made to bring that eerie, chilling vibe into your everyday life. We love all things retro, gory, and nostalgic, making sure every item is as KILLER as the originals that inspired it.
